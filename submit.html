📋 Submission Guidelines

  • Make sure you have verified the notation is accurate before submitting.
  • Use standard Western note names (C D E F G A B) or Solfège (Do Re Mi…).
  • Include lyrics if possible — it helps other musicians follow along.
  • All submissions are reviewed by our team before going live (usually within 24 hours).
